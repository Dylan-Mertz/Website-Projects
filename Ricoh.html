Diagnosing the Problem

Your printer has now done the thing the everyone dreads the most and has stopped printing. Unfortunately, service is not answering and you need assistance immediately. You stumbled across this website and are hoping the issue is an easy fix. As long as you following the troubleshooting steps, that question will be answered fairly quickly.

1. Restart and Unplug the Machine
The first step in every troubleshooting process should be to power down the machine completely and unplug the machine. Fortunately, Ricoh machines are fully rebooted by pressing and holding the red power button on the ride side of tray one under a plastic cover. Once powered off, unplug the machine and leave it off for 30 seconds before powering it back on. Please do not unplug the machine unless it is fully powered down because there is a chance you could accidentally reformat the hard drive. Reformatting the hard drive will wipe the address book and all saved settings on the machine. If issues persist, please check the troubleshooting steps below.
2. Diagnosing the Problem
Now that you attempted to give the machine a full reboot, let's make sure the issue is something you can fix. Start by grabbing the IP address of the machine and typing the number into your web browser. If the webpage does not load, please contact your IT department as there may be an issue with the machine's network connectivity. Otherwise, the printer's web interface should show up and show you what errors may be present. Match the error with the following topics available on this page and there should be a guide to resolve the issue. If you do not see the error in any of the following topics, please call service IMMEDIATELY!!

Replacing the Toner on Your Machine

Open the front door panel.

Pull out the color that you need replaced.

Put the new one in and push it into place. Then close the door.

Run a few jobs and test the quality.

Emptying the Waste Toner box on Your Machine

Open the door panel on the left hand side of tray one.

Put gloves on and double bag your trash can.

Pull out the waste toner box.

Pour the toner out of the spout, under the sliding door at the top, until the box is completely empty.

You can tell the box is completely empty by ensuring the rubber bubble like contraption on the top is completely deflated.

Reinstall the waste toner box and close the door.

Run a few jobs and test the quality.

How to Remove Vertical Lines on Your Copies

Lift the Automatic Document Feeder.

Take your finger and rub it along the slit glass to the left of the main glass.

Using a glass cleaning solution, clean the slit glass.

Close the ADF. Run a few jobs and test the quality.

How to Replace the Paper on Your Machine

Pull open your tray and open the guides all the way.

Remove the paper from the plastic.

Fan the paper good.

Run a few jobs and test the quality!

How to Change the Paper Type on Your Machine

On your machine, select the "Settings" application.

Then click "Paper Tray Settings".

Then Click the Paper Type/Size Option.

Find and select the paper tray that you want to change, then change it to the paper type you want.

Run a job to make sure there are no errors.
